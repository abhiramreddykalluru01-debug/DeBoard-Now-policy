Cookie & Tracking Policy

Last updated: November 28, 2025

What are cookies?

“Cookies” are small text files stored on your device by websites and apps to remember information. In mobile apps, similar small identifiers are used (advertising ID, analytics identifiers).

Which tracking do we use?

  • AdMob (Google) — advertising ID for personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • Analytics — anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance (optional).

Why we use them

To show relevant ads and to measure app performance so we can improve user experience and stability.

Opt-out / Controls

You can opt-out of personalized ads in your device Google account settings. To prevent tracking, adjust your device privacy controls.
